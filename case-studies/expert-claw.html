Summary

An AI cockpit that keeps the human expert in command

ExpertClaw is an AI-orchestrated expert cockpit built during Asurion's annual hackathon. While the customer hears a normal conversation, multiple automation layers work in parallel: real-time speech-to-intent, background CRM lookups, live screen observation, sentiment tracking, and intelligent next-best-action coaching all feed a single decision surface.

The expert stays in command. AI eliminates the cognitive overload of juggling tools so the expert can focus on empathy and speed. The system suggests, the expert delivers. Resolution comes first; recommendations are timed after trust is established.

Why This Matters

Five principles that shaped the design

Execution, Not Suggestions

ExpertClaw completes real support work in the background, reducing manual effort and accelerating resolution.

Real-Time Orchestration at Scale

Conversation AI, tool execution, screen reading, and decisioning run in parallel to keep interactions fast and consistent.

Context-Aware Technical Resolution

Guidance is generated from live customer device context, enabling precise and higher-confidence troubleshooting.

Agent Simplicity, Enterprise Consistency

One Next Best Action experience lowers cognitive load and standardizes quality across teams.

Trust-First Growth

Resolution comes first; recommendations are timed after issue resolution to improve CSAT and conversion together.
