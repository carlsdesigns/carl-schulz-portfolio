Systemic Patterns Found

Authentication friction

Customers re-authenticate repeatedly. Security practices vary between teams.

Data gaps

Customers repeat themselves because data isn't shared across touchpoints.

Inconsistent sales practices

Pitches are difficult to understand and often misleading to customers.

Claims value issues

Upgrades delivered negative value. Replacements didn't match device type.

Support access problems

Partner channel customers need passcodes to transfer. Benefit usage isn't facilitated.
