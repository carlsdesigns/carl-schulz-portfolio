Summary

From pilot support calls to a lifecycle platform vision

Asurion launched a hardware support pilot for Xbox customers who struggled to reach human help. Xbox had moved most support to AI, making it nearly impossible for gamers with real hardware problems to get assistance. Our pilot proved that high-quality human triage not only resolved issues but created a channel for selling Asurion's protection products.

The success of that pilot led us to seek a deeper partnership: embedding Asurion's support and protection into the core Game Pass product, extending across the full lifecycle from purchase through multi-year engagement. Through extensive experience audits and customer research, we shaped a vision demo that became the blueprint for expanding the partnership.

My Role

Senior Director of Product Design

I led strategy and delivery as Senior Director of Product Design, with two designers supporting phased work across the year. I personally ran the quantitative research when it had to ship on a compressed holiday timeline. Experience Research

Mapping the Xbox journey to find where Asurion fits

We audited the complete Xbox ecosystem to understand how it works, where pain points live, and where deeper partnership integration creates value for both gamers and Xbox.

Cloud gaming journey map. End-to-end mapping of the cloud gaming onboarding process, identifying pain points and opportunities at every step.
Onboarding experience audit. Documented the current Xbox onboarding flow to understand where friction and drop-off occur for new users.
Onboarding audit detail. Screen-by-screen analysis of the setup process, surfacing gaps where expert support could reduce abandonment.
Controller setup gap. Smart TV gamers only discover they need a controller after paying for Game Pass, creating frustration and potential churn at the first session.
Game Pass sales opportunity. Xbox has an opportunity to provide live sales support during the enrollment flow, increasing conversion and reducing confusion.
Protection upsell moment. Xbox has a natural opportunity to surface Asurion Care for Xbox during enrollment, when customers are already thinking about their investment.
New gamer game selection. Many first-time gamers don't know what to play. This is an opportunity for guided onboarding that increases time-to-first-session and engagement.
Parental controls complexity. Parents need support configuring safety settings. This is a high-value touchpoint where expert help builds trust and reduces downstream support calls.
