What I Believe

The principles that guide how I lead, design, and build.

Less is more
The most elegant solutions emerge when you strip away everything that doesn't serve the user.
Users have the answers
Great design starts with listening. Research isn't a phase—it's a practice woven into every decision.
Light a fire in others
The best teams aren't managed—they're inspired. I lead by example and invest deeply in growing people.
Complexity deserves elegance
The hardest problems don't need complex solutions. Human-centered design reveals the simplest path forward.
